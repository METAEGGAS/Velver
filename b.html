Bonus conditions

General info

  1. Bonuses are benefits that Optnex credits to traders.
  2. Bonuses are not Optnex's financial obligation to traders.
  3. Traders can get a bonus in a promotion via promo code, by making a deposit with a bonus in the Cashier, or as a status benefit.

Trading turnover

  1. Withdrawals become available after completing a mandatory trading turnover.
  2. The amount of trading turnover depends on the size of the bonus: bonus amount multiplied by the leverage factor. A leverage factor can be specified in the bonus. If it's not specified, then for bonuses less than 50% of the deposit, the leverage is 35. For other bonuses (including no-deposit bonuses), the leverage is 40.
  3. Both successful and unsuccessful trades count toward trading turnover, but only the asset's profitability is taken into account — the investment amount itself is not included.
  4. Trades with zero results in FTT mechanics don't count toward trading turnover.

Limits and cancellation

  1. Bonuses can be limited for activation and use. If you don't activate or use them before the expiration date, they will be canceled.
  2. The bonus will also be canceled if the real account balance drops below the minimum investment amount, or the required trading turnover is not reached.
  3. Traders can cancel a bonus when they have no active trades. The bonus amount will be removed from their total balance.
